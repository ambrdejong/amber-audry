Rhythm (2018)

Spoken word video performance

04’02”

‘Rhythm’ is the result is of a weeklong workshop on rhythm and repetition. The closing assignment was to make something with this piece of mdf board. The result is a performative spoken word video where parts from different texts get connected into something new, while the person reciting this visibly struggles with the weight of the mdf board.

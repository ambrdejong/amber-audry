Questions, Memory, Tree (2021)

Video

07'02"

Questions, Memory, Tree uses windows as a medium of story telling. A window exists between the in and outside, offering a glimpse out as well as inside. The reflection in the window challenges this, giving us a clue about what is going on on either side. Combined with text the video explores uncertanties and that what is unknown to us.
